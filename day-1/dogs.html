How to Care for Long-Haired Dogs

Does your dog have a lot of hair? Is your furry friend a Collie, a Terrier, a Spanial, or something similiar? Are you overwhelmed with the amount of grooming it takes to keep your furry friend looking good? Then look no further. Let's explore potential solutions to your dog's hair problem.

Solutions to Manage your Long-Haired Dog

Use a Quality Comb or Brush

Regular brushing with a quality comb or brush such as the one below.
Hair Comb for Long-Haired Dogs

Trimming Hair

Trimming your dog's hair at regular intervals keeps your furry friend's hair from matting.

Wash with Speciality Shampoo

Dogs need bathes just like we do, just not as often. When bathing your dog, consider using a shampoo formulated
for long-haired dogs or specifically designed to control shedding.
